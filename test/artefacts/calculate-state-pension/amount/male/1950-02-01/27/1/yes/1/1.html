Your State Pension age is 65 years.

You can now claim your State Pension if you haven’t already done so.

Once you claim your State Pension, the Pension Service will work out your full State Pension. This calculator gives you an estimate of your basic State Pension.

Your State Pension estimate

You have enough qualifying years to get a full basic State Pension of £113.1 per week.

How much State Pension you get depends on how many years of National Insurance contributions you have.
