Bus pass

The date you qualify for a bus pass depends on where you live:

  • on 1 February 2010 in England
  • when you turn 60 years old in Scotland,Wales,Northern Ireland

The dates may be different in some areas (including London), check with your council when you can apply for a bus pass.

You need to check with Transport for London if you live in Greater London.
