State Pension calculator

You’ll reach State Pension age on 6 February 2018.

Your State Pension age is 65 years.

You should get your pension claim pack shortly before you reach your State Pension age.

Once you claim your State Pension, the Pension Service will work out your full State Pension.

The State Pension will change on 6 April 2016. You will claim the new State Pension when you reach your State Pension age.

Pension Credit

You might get Pension Credit if you’re retired and on a low income.

You’ll reach the Pension Credit qualifying age on 6 January 2016.

The State Pension age is regularly reviewed and may change in the future. This may also affect Pension Credit.

Bus pass

The date you qualify for a bus pass depends on where you live:

  • on 6 January 2016 in England
  • when you turn 60 years old in Scotland,Wales,Northern Ireland

The dates may be different in some areas (including London), check with your council when you can apply for a bus pass.

You need to check with Transport for London if you live in Greater London.
