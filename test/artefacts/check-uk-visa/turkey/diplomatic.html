Check if you need a UK visa

Diplomatic Business

You won’t need a visa to come to the UK for a posting or to transit through the UK if you’re going to or returning from a posting in another country. You can still apply for an exempt vignette.

Your partner or family members may need a visa to join you.

Government business

You won’t need a visa to come to the UK on official government business for your country if you’re a serving government minister. You can still apply for an exempt vignette.

Your partner or family members may need a visa to join you.
