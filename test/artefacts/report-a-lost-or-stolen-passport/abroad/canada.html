Cancel a lost or stolen passport

You must report your lost or stolen passport

Download form LS01 - ‘Lost or stolen passport notification’

Fill in form LS01 and post it to your nearest British embassy, high commission or consulate.

You can use the online enquiry form to contact Her Majesty’s Passport Office for general advice.

Get a replacement passport

If you don’t need to travel urgently, you can apply for a replacement passport from the country you’re in.

Emergency travel documents

If you need to travel urgently, you may be able to get an emergency travel document from the Embassy, High Commission or Consulate. You will have to pay a fee.
