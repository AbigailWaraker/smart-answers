Check if documents can be legalised

Doctor’s letter (medical)

Your doctor’s letter must be signed by a doctor registered with the General Medical Council.

Your document can’t be legalised if it has been signed on behalf of your doctor by another doctor or member of staff.

Get your documents legalised

You can get your documents legalised by the Legalisation Office if they meet the standards above.
