Maternity and paternity calculator for employers

Statutory Paternity Leave

The employee is not entitled to Statutory Paternity Leave because they don’t have an employment contract with you.

Write to them within 28 days of their leave request confirming this.

Statutory Paternity Pay (SPP)

The employee is entitled to SPP.

Their average weekly earnings are: £346.15

SPP calculation

Date SPP amount
4 January 2015 £78.96
1 February 2015 £197.41
  Total SPP: £276.37

This calculator doesn’t check eligibility for Shared Parental Leave and Pay.

The latest date for them to claim SPP is usually 28 days before they want it to start.
