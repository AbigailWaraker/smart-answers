UK benefits if you're going or living abroad

There is a social security agreement between the UK and Guernsey. You may be able to get Child Benefit abroad.

Contact HMRC to find out if you can get Child Benefit in Guernsey.

You may also be able to claim Guardian’s Allowance, if you’re responsible for a child whose parents have died and you’re getting Child Benefit for them.
