UK benefits if you're going or living abroad

You can’t claim ESA. If you were receiving Incapacity Benefit while living in Jamaica, you may be asked to transition from Incapacity Benefit to ESA (contributory).

You may also be able to get benefits from Jamaica if you’ve paid into a local social security insurance scheme. Contact the local authorities to find out more.
