UK benefits if you're going or living abroad

You should be able to get Child Benefit for a child or children living with you.

You may also be able to claim Guardian’s Allowance, if you’re looking after a child whose parents have died and you’re getting Child Benefit for them.

Contact HMRC to get Child Benefit or Guardian’s Allowance.
