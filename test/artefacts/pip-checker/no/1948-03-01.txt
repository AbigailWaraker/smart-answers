

If you’ve received Disability Living Allowance (DLA) within the past year, you can:

* make a [new claim for DLA](/dla-disability-living-allowance-benefit/eligibility) if you were born on or before 8 April 1948

* [claim Personal Independence Payment (PIP)](/pip) if you were born after 8 April 1948

If you haven’t received DLA within the past year, you can claim [Attendance Allowance](/attendance-allowance) instead.

*[PIP]: Personal Independence Payment
*[DLA]: Disability Living Allowance
