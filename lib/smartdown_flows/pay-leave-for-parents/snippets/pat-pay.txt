##Paternity pay

The mother’s partner can get up to 2 weeks of [statutory paternity pay](/paternity-pay-leave/pay).

###Extra help

They could also get:

- benefits while they’re not working
- Child Tax Credits (after the baby is born)
- Child Benefit (after the baby is born)
