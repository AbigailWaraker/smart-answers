##Shared Parental Leave (partner only)

The primary adopter’s partner can take [shared parental leave](/shared-parental-leave-and-pay) (SPL). It lasts for up to 52 weeks, minus any weeks of adoption leave taken by the primary adopter. 

The partner can take SPL in up to three blocks, going back to work between the blocks.

^The primary adopter can’t take SPL.^
