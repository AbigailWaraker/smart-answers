##Shared Parental Leave (primary adopter only)

The primary adopter can take [shared parental leave](/shared-parental-leave-and-pay) (SPL). It lasts for up to 52 weeks, minus any weeks of adoption leave taken by the primary adopter. 

They can take SPL in up to three blocks, going back to work between the blocks.

^The primary adopter’s partner can’t take SPL.^
