##Adoption pay

The primary adopter can get up to 39 weeks of [adoption pay](/adoption-pay-leave/pay).

 | Dates and amounts
- | -

First 6 weeks | %{rate_of_sap(salary_1)}

Tax and National Insurance will be deducted. 

%The primary adopter’s employer will confirm if they qualify and how much they’ll get.%

###Extra help

The primary adopter could also get:

* benefits while they’re not working
* Child Tax Credits
* Child Benefit

Use a [benefits calculator](/benefits-calculators) to check what benefits or tax credits the primary adopter can get.
