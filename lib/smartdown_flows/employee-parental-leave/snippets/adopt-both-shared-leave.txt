##Shared parental leave

The primary adopter and their partner can take [shared parental leave](/shared-parental-leave-and-pay) (SPL). It lasts for up to 52 weeks, minus any weeks of adoption leave taken by the primary adopter.

The parents can choose how to share this leave between them. They can each take SPL in up to three blocks, going back to work between the blocks.
