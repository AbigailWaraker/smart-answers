##Adoption leave

The primary adopter can take up to 52 weeks of Statutory Adoption Leave.

Leave | Key dates
- | -
Ordinary Adoption Leave (first 26 weeks) | !{date_leave_1} to !{end_of_ordinary_leave(date_leave_1)}
Additional Adoption Leave (last 26 weeks) | !{start_of_additional_leave(date_leave_1)} to !{%end_of_additional_leave(date_leave_1)}
Earliest leave can start | !{earliest_start(placement_date)}
Latest date to give notice | !{qualifying_week(match_date)}

The primary adopter could get more leave if their employer has a company adoption leave scheme.
