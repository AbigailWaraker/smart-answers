# Employment

Read about [employment statuses](/employment-status) if you aren’t sure which one applies.

# What’s the employment status of the mother or primary adopter?

The primary adopter is the person who wants to take adoption leave.

[choice: employment_status_1]
* employee: Employee contract
* worker: Worker contract
* agency: Agency worker
* self-employed: Self-employed
* unemployed: Unemployed

# What’s the employment status of the mother’s or primary adopter’s partner?

The partner may get a short period of leave and be able to share the maternity or adoption leave.

[choice: employment_status_2]
* employee: Employee contract
* worker: Worker contract
* agency: Agency worker
* self-employed: Self-employed
* unemployed: Unemployed
