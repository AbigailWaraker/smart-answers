# Is the tenant renting the property as their main home?

This includes:

- tenancy agreement
- a lease
- a licence
- sub-lease or sub-tenancy
- lodgers
- paying house guests 
- a family member with the tenant as their main home

[choice: main_home]
* yes: Yes
* no: No

* main_home is 'yes' => tenant_over_18
* main_home is 'no' => property_type
